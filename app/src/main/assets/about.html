Extreme Maths Grade 12th

This app is all about Extreme Maths of Grade 12th This application is designed to help a number of people who want to improve their maths skills using this wonderful guide books

The application was designed and arranged according their chapter from the first chapter which is chapter 10 to chapter 15 in the extreme maths. All the chapter are listed from top to bottom as follows

  • Introduction to Limit and Continuity
  • Sequence & Series
  • Introduction to Differential & calculus
  • Application of Differential & Calculus
  • Integral Calculus
  • Three dimensional Geometry
